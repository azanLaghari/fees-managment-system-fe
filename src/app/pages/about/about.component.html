About Fees Management System

The Fees Management System (FMS) is designed to help educational institutions manage student fees efficiently. It allows users to view student fee details, record payments, generate receipts, and track pending fees.

Key Features:

  • Student fee tracking and management
  • Payment history and receipts
  • Reports generation
  • Secure login and role-based access
  • Responsive and user-friendly design
